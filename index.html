Smart Pot



What is Smart Pot

Smart pot is the new innovation where we convert your traditional pot into a smart pot by attaching our state of the art smart-base which will be attached in the bottom of your pot. It has an in-built pump to water plants and sensors to monitor your plants.





How it works

This base is a combination of sensors, pump, PCB, and our 3D-printed base. The sensors help use to monitor our plant like if there is enough water for the plant, and whether there is sufficient sunlight for the plant. The PCB is custom made for the sole purpose of controlling the circuit. It uses USB port to maintain its power.



Is it Durable

The base is made using ABS-filament in a 3D printer. It can withstand more weight than any other 3D printed products. The components are of fine quality. The pot is replaceable due to its low cost. If any problem occurs, we have our support and service team which will contact our customers and take actions immediately.
